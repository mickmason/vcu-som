Overview

Find out about Master's programs at VCU School of Medicine. The VCU School of Medicine offers a wide range of Master's degree programs in research-based and applied biomedical sciences, public health and other areas.

As a Master's student you will have the opportunity to train under the guidance of faculty mentors and perform research or clinical work while taking advanced courses in a particular degree program.

Most Master's students complete their degrees in 2 years. As a graduate of our Master's programs you will be competitive for further academic training (i.e. Ph.D. or M.D. Programs), or you may pursue laboratory research positions in academia or industry as well as more clinically-oriented careers such as Genetic Counseling and Medical Physics, and job opportunities in education as well as other fields.
